# AI Authoring

Agents can draft `.motion` scenes because the format is readable and compact.

Good prompts should include:

- Canvas size and duration
- The focal subject
- Text copy
- Desired mood
- Asset paths
- Export target

Review generated scenes visually in Motionly and tune timing in the editor.
